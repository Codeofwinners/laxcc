What Is A PEN? In Weed Terms

Navigating the vapor. A definitive breakdown of Vape, Dab, and Wax pens for the modern connoisseur.

01

The Vape Pen

The entry point for most cannabis users. Vape pens are battery-powered devices designed for 510-thread cartridges. Trusted hardware is essential to the experience. A leading 510 thread battery heats oil (distillate, live resin, or CO2 oil) just enough to create vapor without burning it.

  • Universal 510-thread compatibility
  • Simple push-button or draw activation
  • Prefilled cartridges (no loading required)
Pros Discreet, easy to use, huge variety of flavors.
Cons Less potent than dabs; cartridge quality varies.

02

The Dab Pen

For those who chase potency. Unlike vape pens that use prefilled liquid carts, dab pens allow you to load your own solid concentrates directly onto a heating coil. It's a portable rig in your pocket.

  • Uses concentrates (Wax, Shatter, Crumble)
  • Direct coil heating system
  • Similar intensity to a dab rig
Pros Extremely potent, full control over material quality.
Cons Requires cleaning; messy to load on the go.

03

The Wax Pen

Often used interchangeably with dab pens, but strictly speaking, wax pens are designed specifically for waxy concentrates. They bridge the gap between ease of use and high potency, often featuring specialized chambers.

  • Designed for thicker waxy extracts
  • Ceramic or Quartz chambers
  • Often features variable voltage
Pros Great flavor preservation, durable design.
Cons Higher learning curve than standard vapes.

Everything you need to know about cannabis pens, explained by experts.

What is the strongest pen?

Generally, Dab Pens take the crown. Because you load pure concentrates like rosin or shatter directly onto the coil, you get the full, undiluted potency of the extract. Vape pens are limited by the oil's formulation.

Are chargers universal?

Not anymore. While 510-thread batteries are the standard for carts, proprietary pod systems like Plug Play and Stiiizy require their own specific cables. Always check your device's port (USB-C vs Micro-USB).

Can I use flower in a pen?

Only if it's a Dry Herb Vaporizer. Putting flower into a wax pen or oil pen will ruin the heating element immediately. You need a device specifically built with a convection or conduction oven for herbs.

How long do pens last?

A typical 1g cartridge lasts a moderate user about 2 weeks. The battery itself can last 1-2 years with proper care. Disposable pens last until the oil runs out, but are less eco-friendly.
