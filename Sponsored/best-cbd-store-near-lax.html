Is CBD legal to buy near LAX?

Yes. California legalized both hemp-derived CBD (federally legal under the 2018 Farm Bill) and cannabis-derived CBD through its regulated dispensary system. LAXCC is a fully licensed California cannabis retailer, making every purchase legal, compliant, and traceable. Always purchase from a licensed dispensary to ensure the products are lab-tested and legitimate.

What's the difference between CBD and THC?

Both CBD (cannabidiol) and THC (tetrahydrocannabinol) come from the cannabis plant, but they work very differently. THC is psychoactive — it produces the "high" associated with cannabis. CBD is non-intoxicating and is commonly used for general wellness, stress support, and physical recovery without any impairment. Many LAXCC CBD products contain little to no THC.

Can I bring CBD products on a plane from LAX?

The TSA focuses on security threats rather than cannabis searches, but current federal policy still technically prohibits cannabis — including cannabis-derived CBD — from being transported across state lines. Hemp-derived CBD products (0.3% THC or less) occupy a greyer area and are more commonly permitted. We always recommend checking the most current TSA and airline guidelines before traveling, and consulting with our budtenders on the best product options for your situation.
