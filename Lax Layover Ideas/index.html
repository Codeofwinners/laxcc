LAX Layover FAQs

Common questions about leaving the airport during a layover.

Can you leave LAX during a layover?

Yes. Thousands of travelers do it daily. Allow 2 hours (domestic) or 3 hours (international) to get back through security. Use the LAX-it shuttle for faster rideshare pickups.

How long of a layover do you need to leave LAX?

At least 4 hours. This gives you 1.5–2 hours to explore nearby Westchester, grab In-N-Out, or visit local shops before heading back.

What is the closest beach to LAX?

Dockweiler Beach and Playa Del Rey—both about 8–10 minutes by car. Dockweiler has fire pits and incredible plane-watching views.

Is there a dispensary near LAX airport?

LAXCC is at 8332 Lincoln Blvd—5 minutes from the LAX-it rideshare zone. We're the closest premium dispensary to the terminals.
