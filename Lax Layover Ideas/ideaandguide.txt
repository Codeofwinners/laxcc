## The 6-Hour "Coastal Decompression": Playa Del Rey (90293)

With six hours, you have enough time to trade the smell of jet fuel for the scent of salt air. Playa Del Rey (PDR) is the Westside’s best-kept secret—a quiet, upscale beach town that feels worlds away from the airport chaos.

### Seaside Serenity

Have your driver drop you at the end of Culver Blvd. From there, you can walk the **Del Rey Lagoon** or head out onto the jetty. Watching the sailboats drift in and out of the Marina against the backdrop of the Pacific is the ultimate mental reset.

### Local Flavor

For a high-end meal with a local feel, visit **Bacari PDR** for Mediterranean-inspired small plates and a stunning wine list. If you prefer something more historic, **The Shack** or **Prince O’ Whales** offers a classic California "beach bar" atmosphere that has remained unchanged for decades.
