Legal Age: You must be 21 years of age or older to purchase, possess, or consume cannabis edibles in California.

Possession Limits: Adults can possess up to one ounce (28.5 grams) of cannabis flower or eight grams of cannabis concentrate.

THC Limits: Edible products are limited to 10mg of THC per serving and 100mg of THC per package.

Consumption Restrictions: Consuming cannabis in public places is generally prohibited. Consumption is also restricted in areas where smoking is banned.

Packaging and Labeling: Products must be in child-resistant containers and clearly labeled with THC content, health warnings, and batch numbers.
